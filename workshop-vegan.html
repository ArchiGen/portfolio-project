Vegan Cooking Workshop

Vegan Cooking Workhsop.


Class duration: 4h (Friday 18.00-20.00 Saturday 9.00-11.00)
Price: $250 per person

This vegan Masterclass Taste & Texture teaches you how to become a (better) vegan chef. You will be able to take your vegan dishes to the next level. You will learn how to add new & satisfying flavours and aromas to your dishes with insight on umami-rich ingredients, insight on the Maillard reaction & by a better understanding of the workings of our taste perception. All techniques and ideas within this course are incorporated in 5+ clearly written and unique recipes.
